About the Game

OMORI is a surreal indie RPG developed by OMOCAT. It tells a deeply emotional story about depression, trauma, friendship, and self-discovery. The game is known for its pixel-art visuals, haunting music, and multiple endings depending on player choices.

Gameplay

The game combines traditional turn-based RPG mechanics with unique psychological elements. Players navigate both a colorful dream world and a bleak reality, solving puzzles, battling enemies, and uncovering secrets. Emotions like Happy, Sad, and Angry play a crucial role in battles.

Main Characters

  • Omori - The silent protagonist who explores both dreams and reality.
  • Sunny - The real-world identity of Omori.
  • Kel - A loyal and energetic friend.
  • Aubrey - A girl with a tough exterior and a soft heart.
  • Hero - The older brother figure, gentle and calm.
  • Basil - A shy boy who holds a dark secret.
